Data Readiness Profiler

Drop in a representative data sample and get the Coverage & Credibility evidence for a 5C assessment — fill rate, duplicates, type consistency, distributions, and freshness — computed entirely on your machine.

🔒 Your data never leaves your browser. This page makes no network calls with your file — parsing and all statistics run locally in JavaScript. Only the aggregate summary (counts and rates, never raw rows) is meant to be copied back into the DRA-5C agent.
